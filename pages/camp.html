The Blood Donation Process

The blood donation process from the time you arrive until the time you leave takes about an hour.

The donation itself is only about 8-10 minutes on average.

Registration and Medical Screening

* Check in for your donation by scanning your ID or enteing your information and be welcomed into the donation waiting area.

* We'll identif you using your ID ,such as your driver's license. Other acceptable forms of ID include: milot immigration and naturalization card (green card) .

* You'll be asked to comfirm your name ,date of birth,adress and other demographic information.

* We'll check your temperature , pulse , blood pressure and hemoglobin level.

* During a confidental interview, some questions will be asked like , about your health history, places you have traveled, medications that maybe in your system and questions to assess infectious disease risk.

* After donating, you'll have a snack and something to drink in the refreshment area.

* You'll leave after approximately 15 minutes and continue your normal routine.

* Enjoy the feeling of accomplishment knowing you are helping to save lives.

* Take a selfie or simple share your review in our feedback page . It may inspire other people to become blood donor.
